Introducing: SQUEEZIT Flavour Cards

June 12, 2023

Hello there, my name is Smarty Arty Orange - one of the flavours here at Squeezit. I have been given the ultimate honour of announcing the debut of myself and all of my friends! We are proud and excited to announce our brand new Squeezit Flavour Cards. You might be asking yourself: how does this work? Well, just buy any official Squeezit drink product and you might receive a special card underneath the cap of the bottle!

We know many have been waiting a long time for us to come back, and we wanted to treat our extra nostalgic fans with something special to remember our newly redesigned Squeezit Characters by! That isn't all; we might just be planning on creating more flavours in the future, with even more delicious characters...

(Luckily, we have extra smart fruit like me to come up with the best ones).

But for now, you can enjoy my four best friends: Grumpy Grape, Silly Strawberry, Chucklin' Cherry, and Smarty Arty Orange.

The process of developing these wonderfully sweet characters involved a lot of late-night fruit adventures - stories that we will save for later. Perhaps even my pal Silly Strawberry will be able to tell you themself one day if they get the opportunity to write in our blog like me!

Well, that's all I wanted to say today. Being so smart takes up a lot of my time, you know.

- Smarty Arty Orange
